About Us

Welcome to BB & Emma.N Civils! With 8 years of dedicated experience, we specialize in steel reinforcement, precast panels, and formwork. Our expertise spans various infrastructure projects, including high-rises, highways, bridges, columns, beams, slabs, and labour provision. We pride ourselves on delivering top-quality work on time and within budget. Our civil teams are dynamic, skilled and have excellent experience in the trades we service. We take pride in delivering successful projects on time, within budget and to the highest quality standards. We have a large team and human resource that ensures capacity for and continuity of all projects, from small to large. A company-wide training regime not only ensures quality at all levels, but effectively up-skills and uplifts our workers and the community we work with. Our clients include governments local authorities corporate groups and property developers.
